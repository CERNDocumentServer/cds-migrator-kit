Seventy years of reorganisation

Every department, division and sector CERN has run since its statutes came into force on 29 September 1954, reconstructed from the SIS historical organisation charts and grouped by what each unit actually did.

Reconstructed from decade-by-decade organisation charts published by CERN's Scientific Information Service; several code/name pairs (AT, EP, PS) were reused for unrelated units at different points, and boundary years are approximate where the source charts overlap two calendar years. Treat this as a reading of the archive, not a primary record — consult the original charts for anything citable.
